## A Win-Win Solution

Comedor La Esperanza, located in the heart of Santo Domingo, serves over 200 meals daily to families in need. When we approached them about our dog rescue initiative, owner Maria Fernandez immediately saw the symbiotic potential.

### The Problem They Faced

Like many restaurants and comedors, La Esperanza struggled with food waste disposal. Scraps, leftover rice, and vegetable trimmings would either go to landfill or require costly disposal services.

### Our Solution

We placed a shelter just 20 meters from their back door. Now, instead of throwing away food scraps, Maria sets them out in clean bowls each evening. Three street dogs now have a reliable meal every day—and Maria has zero food waste.

## Community Impact

The best part? The neighborhood has embraced this arrangement. Locals who once complained about stray dogs now see them as part of the community. Children learn compassion, adults see practical sustainability in action, and everyone wins.
