## A Community Effort

Last week, we completed our first insulated dog shelter in Santo Domingo. This milestone represents months of planning, community outreach, and hard work from our local builder, Carlos Rodriguez.

### The Build Process

Using over 200 recycled plastic bottles, palm fiber insulation, and locally-sourced rope, Carlos constructed a weatherproof home that will protect street dogs from both the intense Caribbean sun and sudden tropical rainstorms.

The integrated rainwater collection system ensures our four-legged friends always have access to clean drinking water—a game-changer during the dry season.

### What's Next?

This is just the beginning. We have plans for 10 more shelters across Santo Domingo this year, each strategically placed near local comedors (community kitchens) where restaurant partners provide food waste to feed the dogs.
